Launch Module

The launch module provided an opportunity to become familiar with the virtual learning enviroment while providing an oppourtunity for students to develop their academic writing skills for the first assignment.

Academic writing

During the launch module I developed my skills in academic writing. Among other skills I learnt how use harvard referrencing and how to use tools such as Turnitin to avoid accidental plagarism. For the induction assignment I wrote an essay on "The importance of a PG Cert in computer sceince". Writing this essay I followed the steps:

  • Read question and mark scheme
  • Search Google schoolars for releavnt papers
  • Read the relevant papers and collect many references and Ideas that could be talked about
  • Write the introduction and other key points specified in the breif
  • Trim back the collected ideas and references to form the core arguments
  • Adjust the document to meet the Unit 1 & 2 guidence
  • Ittereate over the document tweaking order to make sure their is a contunious flow for the reader
  • Ask relative to proof read
  • Make grammer adjustemnts for highlighted regions
  • Run the document through a grammerly check
  • Finalize document
I think that the reading a broad collection of sources upfront is a good way to prepare the mind for idea synthisis. Next time I would create a list of goals for the assignment for my self. Many of the resources provided in Unit 1 and Unit 2 provided guidence for writing the essay. Next time I would aim to capture this guidence in a list of 'goals' to refer to during the writing process. This would eliminate the need to make adjustements to match the guidence after the essay had been written.
